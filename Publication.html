Keep It Simple: Using README Files to Advance Standardization in Chronobiology

we propose an approach that emphasizes simplicity and practicality: the development of README templates tailored for particular data types and species. Data-Sharing Article:

Data Sharing in Circadian Rhythm and Mental Health Research

This article addresses the challenges of data sharing in circadian rhythm and mental health research, highlighting barriers like data complexity, privacy concerns, and academic culture. We proposed initial recommendations, including fostering a culture of openness, integrating FAIR principles, and providing training, ethical guidelines, and data curation services to improve data-sharing practices in the field.
